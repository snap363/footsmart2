Feel light on your feet in Easy Spirit Women's Traveltime Clogs

Less weight in your shoes translates into less fatigue on your feet
This sporty sneaker clog keeps foot cool and comfortable and fully supported. The removable insole, made of compression-molded ElonÂ®, secures and cushions the foot.

Features & Benefits of Easy Spirit Women's Traveltime Clogs:

  • Elon insole provides super-resilient cushioning, much greater than that of standard EVA insoles.
  • Remove insole to accommodate personal orthotics.
  • Mesh in the upper allows air to circulate within the shoe, keeping foot cool and dry.
  • Durable rubber outsole creates excellent traction, helping to secure your steps.
  • Clog styling allows you to step in and go.

Other Important Information About Easy Spirit Women's Traveltime Clogs:

  • Material Content: suede and mesh upper, jersey upper lining, polyester sockliner, foam insole, EVA midsole, rubber outsole.
  • Care: Lightly brush with a soft to medium brush.
  • Imported.
